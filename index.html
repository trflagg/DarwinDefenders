Darwin Defenders v0.1

By hi-scor.es

Instructions

  • Control your ship with the mouse.
  • Click the mouse to shoot.
  • You start with a randomly placed gun & shield.
  • After each successful wave you gain another random piece.
  • After each wave, a genetic algorithm selects the best enemies and cross-breeds them to produce the next generation of enemies.
  • Each game is different. Watch out because the enemies will evolve to exploit your weaknesses.

Enjoy!
